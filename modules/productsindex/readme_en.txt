Drag&Drop Products Positions  in Category/Brand + Sort Prestashop Module

DESCRIPTION:
Edit products positions in Categories and Brands, Drag&Drop products display order by mouse. Sort products by Position, stock(quantity), name, price, date update, фтв ID.

INSTALLATION:
1. Upload module to your shop (module will be auto installed in ps > 1.7.0) or signin to your Prestashop Addons account in admin panel and click install.
2. For ps 1.5-1.6: choose not installed modules and filter by author "ZLab Solutions". Click "install".
3. You can access module interface by clicking "configure" over module or clicking tab “Catalog” -> "Product Positions" in main menu.
